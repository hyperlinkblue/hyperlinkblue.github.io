About Me

I'm a university student with a passion for web development, game development, vocal synthesis, and music production.

My journey into technology began in early 2022 when I started creating and hosting my own websites at 15 years old. Through communities centered around the indie web (particularly platforms like Neocities), I discovered the joy of building personal spaces on the internet and learning how the web works beyond social media platforms and algorithms.

Since then, I've continued exploring different creative and technical fields. I particularly enjoy making more webpages, experimenting with game development, exploring niche corners of the web, and creating my own voicebanks & original songs in my free time.

My long-term goal is to pursue a career as both a game developer and music producer, creating interactive and artistic experiences that bring together programming, storytelling, sound, and design.
